Bias-aware design checklist

Before you publish
  • Use clear heading hierarchy (screen readers rely on it).
  • Check contrast + avoid meaning by color alone.
  • Write alt text that describes what matters.
When you choose examples / “default users”
  • Avoid assuming one identity is “normal.”
  • Include examples across abilities + contexts.
  • Ask: who benefits, and who gets excluded?
When you embed media
  • Add captions when possible (or a short text summary).
  • Make video responsive so it works on mobile.
  • Ensure keyboard users can reach everything.

Featured concept: intersectionality

Intersectionality reminds us people don’t experience the world through one identity at a time. Identities overlap, and systems can treat those overlaps differently.

We connect this idea to both our resources (Implicit Bias Guide) and our tech heroes (Tech Hero page).
